50 Cent

Curtis James Jackson III, also known as 50 Cent, while most notable for his career as a rapper and singer, has involved himself in the film industry. He has acted in, written, and produced several movies. These include Get Rich or Die Tryin' (2005), Southpaw (2015), and Den of Thieves: Pantera (2025). He has also starred on TV shows. On Metacritic, he has an average score of 50 for movies and 64 for TV shows.
