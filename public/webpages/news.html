Donald Trump Proposes His Face on $1 Coins

Donald Trump has done much for the United States of America. Whether those were for the better or for the worse is up for debate. Another Trump policy might take place, and it might be in American pockets and wallets by next year. The Trump administration has proposed a new design for $1 coins: an issue with Trump's face on them. This is in spite of a precedent declaring that no presidents, sitting or former, may appear on currency.

King Charles III Says That His Cancer Treatment Will Be Reduced

In February 2024, the current reigning monarch of England, King Charles III, revealed he had been diagnosed with cancer. While the type of cancer was not revealed, the King has declared that, thanks to "early diagnosis" and "effective intervention", his treatment will be reduced next year. King Charles had announced this in a speech via video message broadcast, played for the Stand Up to Cancer campaign.

Taylor Swift's End of an Era Docuseries Has Come Out

Last year, on December 8, 2024, Taylor Swift ended her record-breaking The Eras Tour that celebrated her artistic journey as a singer. It is the first and only tour so far to gross $1 billion and $2 billion dollars, making it the highest-grossing tour of all time. It had an enormous global impact, sparked a revival of friendship bracelets, and certified Swift as a superstar pop icon. And, now, she has released a docuseries detailing how everything went down.

Delivery Van Crashes Into a Jarkata School, Injuring 21 Total

Tragedy struck SDN Kalibaru 01 elementary school in Jakarta after a delivery van had crashed through the school gates and injured 21 people, consisting of students and teachers. The delivery van was delivering the free meals for the school, as part of Indonesia's Makan Bergizi Gratis (Free Nutritious Meals) program. The driver has claimed that his brakes were not working, and in a panic, stepped on the accelerator.
